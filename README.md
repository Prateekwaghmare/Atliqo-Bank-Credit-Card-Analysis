# AtliQo Bank Credit Card Launch Analysis

---

## Executive Summary

AtliQo Bank plans to launch a new credit card in a competitive financial services market. Management identified potential growth opportunities within younger demographics but required data-driven validation of product-market fit and customer targeting strategy.

This analysis evaluates customer demographics, spending behavior, credit maturity, and digital payment adoption patterns to determine:

- Whether growth potential lies in pricing optimization or credit access expansion  
- Which demographic segment offers the highest lifetime value potential  
- Where lending capacity and credit-building gaps exist  
- Whether the new youth-focused credit card performs significantly better than the existing product
